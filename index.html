Spooky Run

Controls

Press "ArrowUp" or "W" to jump

Press "ArrowLeft" or "A" to move left

Press "ArrowRight" or "D" to move right

Press "ArrowDown" or "S" to sit when player is on ground

Press "ArrowDown" or "S" to dive when player is in air

Press "Space Bar" to roll and attack!

Score 30 points within 30 seconds to win

Press Enter to start the game!
